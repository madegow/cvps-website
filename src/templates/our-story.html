The Trust is also having its plan to come up with residential school and old age homes in the campus.

The future generation should be prepared for handling global challenges and make use of international opportunities. The need of the hour is to develop a competitive edge with global perspective. We personally feel that we need to pay special attention especially to the rural children because of the environment they live in and the challenges they go through. Keeping this in mind, the management has architected its vision statement and committed to deliver.

The Vision of Chirantana is “To Encourage the children to explore their innate potential with full Freedom being Good to others and environment by Helping them to become global citizens”.

Finally, We would like to quote a sloka in Sanskrit, which we believe in its true essence.

“ನಾಸ್ತಿ ವಿದ್ಯ ಸಮೋ ಬಂಧುಹು ನಾಸ್ತಿ ವಿದ್ಯ ಸಮಃ ಸಹೃತ್ ನಾಸ್ತಿ ವಿದ್ಯ ಸಮಂ ವಿತ್ತಂ ನಾಸ್ತಿ ವಿದ್ಯ ಸಮಂ ಸುಖಂ” ಅರ್ಥಾತ್ “ವಿದ್ಯೆಗೆ ಸಮನಾದ ಬಂಧುಗಳಿಲ್ಲ, ವಿದ್ಯೆಗೆ ಸಮನಾದ ಸ್ನೇಹಿತರಿಲ್ಲ, ವಿದ್ಯೆಗೆ ಸಮನಾದ ಆಸ್ತಿಯಿಲ್ಲ, ವಿದ್ಯೆಗೆ ಸಮನಾದ ಸುಖವಿಲ್ಲ”
