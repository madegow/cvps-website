    Message From Management

    On behalf of the Chirantana Valley Public School Board of Trustees, we extend a very warm welcome to you and your family as you embark on that very important decision as to where to educate your child. It is with a great sense of determination, self-satisfaction and a bit of pride that we dedicate Chirantana Valley Public School (CVPS) to the people in and around Kabbalu. CVPS is managed by Chirantana Educational and Social Trust®, having its head office in Bengaluru, with an ambitious goal of providing quality, Value based education to the rural mass by creating a friendly environment with all required facilities in a 15 Acre beautiful campus. The Trust is also having its plan to come up with residential school and old age homes in the campus.
